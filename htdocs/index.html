Acinon Online Organisation

We make stuff, because that's what we do best.

(With "best" being a relative term)

We are the Acinon Online Organisation, a group that specialises in weird software and multimedia products and internet projects. I'm not going to pretend that we've done or can do anything revolutionary or important.


Our Mascot

We have a commitment to never going corporate, having fun, and making sure you do too. We've tried to reflect that in our website, but that's a bit hard without making it look absolutely awful. If you want to help out, we have public repos at GitHub. This site is a collection of pages for you to browse to learn more about us and our projects.
